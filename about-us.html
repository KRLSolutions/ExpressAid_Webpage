About Us

Welcome to ExpressAid – Your Trusted Partner in Home Healthcare.

At ExpressAid, we believe healthcare should be immediate, compassionate, and accessible to everyone. That’s why we’ve built a platform that allows you to book a certified nurse at your doorstep within 15 minutes, ensuring timely care when you need it most.

Whether it's post-surgical support, chronic illness management, elder care, injections, or dressing wounds, our qualified nursing professionals deliver hospital-grade care in the comfort and safety of your home. Every service is tailored to your needs—because we know that true healing begins where you feel most secure.

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform home healthcare by providing:

  • Immediate access to qualified nurses
  • Hospital-grade care in the comfort of your home
  • Personalized, compassionate service
  • Transparent, secure, and reliable care

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where every home is a safe haven for recovery and well-being, and to become India’s most trusted and responsive home healthcare provider. We aim to:

  • Empower individuals to take control of their health
  • Provide peace of mind through reliable, professional care
  • Bridge the gap between healthcare and everyday life
  • Ensure that every home is a safe haven for recovery and well-being

Our Approach

Our approach is simple yet powerful:

  • We connect you with the best nurses in your area
  • We ensure every service is delivered with compassion and expertise
  • We provide transparent, secure, and reliable care
  • We empower individuals to take control of their health

Our Team

  • We are a team of dedicated professionals who are passionate about providing the best possible care to our clients
  • We are committed to delivering the highest quality of care to our clients
  • Our certified nurses undergo rigorous background checks and continuous training to maintain the highest clinical standards
  • Behind the scenes, our support and tech teams work round-the-clock to ensure every appointment is smooth, secure, and efficient
