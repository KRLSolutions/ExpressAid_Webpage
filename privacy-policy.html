Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 8, 2025

At ExpressAid, your privacy is important to us. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring transparency in how we use it. By accessing or using our website or mobile application (collectively referred to as the “Platform”), you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy outlines how ExpressAid collects, uses, stores, processes, shares, and protects your information. If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not access or use the Platform.

1. Applicability

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Platform, including both visitors and registered users. It governs how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you interact with our services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect information that can identify you, such as your name, email address, phone number, and address.
  • Health Information: If you provide health-related information, we may collect it to better understand your needs and provide tailored services.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the Platform, including your IP address, browser type, and device information.
  • Payment Information: If you make payments, we collect financial details to process transactions securely.
  • Communications: We may collect information you provide when you contact us, such as your name, email address, and message content.
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and analyze usage patterns.

3. How the Information is Used

We use your information to:

  • Deliver home healthcare services efficiently and safely.
  • Enable communication with nurses, doctors, labs, and support teams.
  • Send service-related updates (e.g., appointment reminders, order status).
  • Improve the quality, personalization, and functionality of our services.
  • Maintain compliance with legal, operational, or contractual obligations.

4. Permissions we may Request

To enhance your experience, we may request permissions for:

  • Location: To assign nearby service providers.
  • Camera/Media/Files: For uploading prescriptions or reports.
  • Microphone/Phone: To facilitate audio/video consultations.
  • SMS/WiFi/Notifications: For verification and service updates

All permissions are requested with clear opt-in consent.

5. Storage and Security

All data is stored securely on servers located in India, protected by SSL encryption and access control protocols. Our systems comply with ISO 27001:2013 standards.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary for service delivery, legal compliance, or as otherwise required. You may request deletion by emailing: support@expressaid.in

7. Sharing of Information

We may share your information:

  • With service providers for delivery and support.
  • With regulatory authorities for compliance audits.
  • In case of legal obligations or safety concerns.
  • With your employer or insurer (if applicable).
  • Never for unsolicited advertising without your consent.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to improve site functionality, security, and user experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

9. User Rights & Consent

By using our services, you:

  • Consent to collection and processing of your data.
  • Confirm you’ve obtained consent to share information of any dependents
  • May withdraw consent at any time, though this may limit service delivery.

10. External Links

Our Platform may include links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies before sharing data.

This Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of our Platform implies consent to the updated terms. Check this page regularly for changes.
